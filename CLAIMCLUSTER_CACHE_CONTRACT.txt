CLAIMCLUSTER_CACHE_CONTRACT

Purpose:
- ClaimClusterCache is a local performance/cache helper only.
- ClaimClusterCache is not a source of truth.
- ClaimClusterCache must not decide whether a matter exists, whether a matter belongs to a lawsuit, or whether search has no results.
- ClaimClusterCache must not be rebuilt from Clio or hydrated from Clio.
- Clio must not be queried to fill or refresh ClaimClusterCache.

Allowed temporary behavior:
- ClaimClusterCache may be read only as an optimization after local ClaimIndex/local matter data is already the source.
- ClaimClusterCache may be written only by a narrowly scoped cache helper, not by search correctness routes.
- ClaimClusterCache may be cleared as cache maintenance.
- app/api/claim-index/refresh-cluster is a quarantined legacy operational route and must remain blocked unless replaced by an explicitly local-only cache maintenance route.

Forbidden behavior:
- Search results must not depend on ClaimClusterCache as the correctness source.
- Zero-result search must not fallback to Clio or ClaimClusterCache as a substitute for local database truth.
- Lawsuit generation must not use ClaimClusterCache as the source for sibling membership.
- Direct matter display/edit routes must not use ClaimClusterCache.
- ClaimClusterCache must not import or call Clio helpers.
- ClaimClusterCache must not contain Clio custom-field logic.

Future migration:
- Prefer local ClaimIndex/local matter queries directly for correctness.
- If performance requires caching later, create a clearly named local search projection/cache with explicit invalidation.
- Delete or rename ClaimClusterCache only in a separate schema migration after backups and restore procedures are confirmed.
